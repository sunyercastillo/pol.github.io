# Images Directory

## Required Images

1. **profile.jpg** - Your profile/portrait photo
   - Recommended size: 400x400px or larger (square ratio works best)
   - Formats: JPG, PNG, or WebP
   - This will appear in the About section

2. **background.jpg** - Hero section background image
   - Recommended size: 1920x1080px or larger
   - Formats: JPG, PNG, or WebP
   - This will be the background of your hero section (main banner)
   - Choose a high-quality image that represents you or your work (landscape, lab, field work, etc.)

## Optional Images

You can add other images for your research or publications as needed.

## Tips

- Use high-quality images for better appearance
- Compress images to keep website loading fast (use tools like tinypng.com)
- You can use free stock photos from unsplash.com or pexels.com as temporary placeholders
